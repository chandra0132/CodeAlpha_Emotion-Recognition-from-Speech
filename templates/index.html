Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) & Dataset Insights

  • Dataset Composition: Analyzed 1,440 high-quality speech clips from the RAVDESS database.
  • Balanced Distribution: Perfectly balanced across 24 actors (12 male, 12 female). Contains 192 samples for calm, happy, sad, angry, fearful, disgust, surprised, and 96 samples for neutral.
  • Waveform Signature: High-arousal/energetic emotions (angry, happy, surprised) exhibit large amplitudes and rapid fluctuations. Subdued/low-arousal emotions (neutral, calm, sad) show softer, smoother signal envelopes.
  • Mel Spectrogram Energy: Angry and fearful speech presents massive frequency energy spanning up to 8 kHz and higher, pointing to sharp vocal quality and shouting. In contrast, sad and neutral spectrograms show energy clustered tightly in lower registers.

Architecture Insight

The Support Vector Machine (SVM) trained on 1D time-averaged acoustic features yields the highest accuracy of 68.1%. Because it uses a Radial Basis Function (RBF) kernel, it finds optimal decision boundaries in high-dimensional spectral feature spaces, avoiding overfitting on a moderately sized dataset.

EDA Observations and Insights:
==============================

1. Dataset Composition: Found 1440 total speech clips.
2. Distribution: The RAVDESS dataset is perfectly balanced across emotions,
   with 192 samples for calm, happy, sad, angry, fearful, disgust, surprised
   (each actor performs 2 repetitions of 2 statements at 2 levels of intensity),
   and 96 samples for neutral (since neutral only has normal intensity).

3. Waveform Observations:
   - Highly energetic emotions (angry, surprised, happy) exhibit higher amplitude peaks
     and wider signal fluctuations compared to lower-energy emotions (neutral, calm, sad).
   - Sadness and calm show a smoother, less abrupt envelope, suggesting softer articulation.

4. Spectrogram Observations:
   - Angry and fearful expressions show substantial energy at higher frequencies (up to 8kHz and beyond),
     reflecting sharper voice quality, shouting, or hyper-articulation.
   - Sad and neutral spectrograms have energy concentrated primarily at lower frequencies
     with minimal high-frequency activation, aligning with their subdued vocal qualities.
   - Disgust shows a distinct harmonic structure and slower transition speeds.
